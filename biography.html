Biography

John Sax is a passionate bass player with a unique sound shaped by influences from jazz, funk, and Afrobeat. Born in Lagos, he began playing the bass guitar at the age of 12 and has since performed across Europe and Africa.

His deep groove and musical intuition make him a sought-after session and live performer. When he's not touring, John teaches bass guitar to aspiring musicians, helping shape the next generation of artists.
